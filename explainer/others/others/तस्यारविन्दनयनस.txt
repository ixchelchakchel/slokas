तस्य - of that (person)
अरविन्दनयनस्य - whose eyes are like lotus petals
पदारविन्दकिञ्जल्क-मिश्र-तुलसी-मकरन्द-वायु:। - the breeze carrying the fragrance of mixed lotus pollen and basil leaves

Meaning:
This verse describes the breeze that carries the fragrance of mixed lotus pollen and basil leaves, which is in turn associated with a person whose eyes resemble the beautiful and delicate petals of a lotus flower. The verse portrays a serene and peaceful atmosphere where nature's delicate elements blend harmoniously with the beauty of a person's eyes.
