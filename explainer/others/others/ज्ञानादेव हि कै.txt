ज्ञानादेव - Through knowledge alone
हि - indeed
कैवल्यम् - liberation, enlightenment

Meaning: Liberation or enlightenment is attained through knowledge alone.

Explanation: This verse emphasizes the importance of knowledge in attaining liberation or enlightenment. It suggests that gaining true knowledge or wisdom is the key to reaching a state of ultimate freedom and liberation from the cycle of birth and death. It implies that knowledge not only provides understanding but also brings about a transformative state of consciousness that leads to liberation.
