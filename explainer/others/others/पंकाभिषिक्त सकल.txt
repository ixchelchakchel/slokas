पंकाभिषिक्त – covered in mud
सकलावयवं – all limbs/body parts
विलोक्य – seeing
दामोदरं – Lord Krishna, who has a cord tied around his waist
वदति – says
कोपवशात्य – due to anger
यशोदा – Yashoda (Krishna's foster mother)
त्वं – you
सूकरोऽसि – you are a pig
गतजन्मनि – in a previous life
पूतनारे – O demon named Putana

Meaning:
Yashoda, in her anger, sees Lord Krishna covered in mud and says, "You are a pig, O demon named Putana who was reborn."
