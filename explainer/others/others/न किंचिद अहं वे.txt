न (na) - not
किंचित् (kimchit) - anything
अहम् (aham) - I
वेदिषं (vedisham) - know

Breakdown:
न (na) - negation, expresses negation or denial
किंचित् (kimchit) - anything, something
अहम् (aham) - I, self, me
वेदिषं (vedisham) - know, understand, comprehend

Meaning:
न किंचिद अहं वेदिषं (na kimchid aham vedisham) translates to "I do not know anything." In this verse, the speaker is expressing their ignorance or lack of knowledge about something in particular. They are confessing that they have no understanding or information about the subject matter being discussed.
