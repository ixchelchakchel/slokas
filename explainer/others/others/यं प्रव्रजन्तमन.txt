यं - whom
प्रव्रजन्तम् - wandering
अनुपेतम् - approached
अपेतकृत्यं - having renounced
द्वैपायनः - Vyasa (another name for the sage)
विरहकातरः - desiring separation
आजुहाव - called out

Meaning: Vyasa, the sage who had renounced worldly attachments, called out to the one who approached him with the desire for wandering and separation.
