एषः: This
आत्मा: self
अपहतपाप्मा: free from sin
विजरः: free from old age
विमृत्युः: free from death
विशोकः: free from sorrow
विजिघत्सुः: free from hunger
अपिपासः: free from thirst

Meaning:
This self is free from sin, old age, death, sorrow, hunger, and thirst.

This verse highlights the qualities of the self, which is described as being free from various afflictions and limitations. It emphasizes the true nature of the self as being pure, immortal, free from suffering, and devoid of worldly desires. The verse suggests that the self is beyond the transient aspects of existence and possesses inherent qualities of purity and immortality.
