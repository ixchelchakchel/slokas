पौलस्त्यं - (paustyam) Pertaining to the descendant of Pulastya (referring to Bhishma, who was born in the lineage of Pulastya)
जयते - (jayate) Wins
हलिं - (halim) For the sake of establishing order and righteousness
कलयते - (kalayate) Creates
कारुण्यमातन्वते - (karunyamatnavate) Extends compassion
म्लेच्छान्मूर्छयते - (mlecchanmurchayate) Overwhelms the enemies or non-believers
दशाकृतिकृते - (dashakrutikrite) Creates ten-fold power(through austerities/penances)
कृष्णाय - (krishnaya) To Lord Krishna
तुभ्यं - (tubhyam) To you
नमः नमः - (namah namah) Salutations, homage
कमलनाभाय - (kamalanabhaya) To the one with a lotus-shaped navel (referring to Lord Vishnu)
कमलमालिने - (kamalamaline) To the one adorned with a garland of lotuses
कमलपादाय - (kamalapadaya) To the one with lotus feet
नमस्ते - (namaste) I bow to you
कमलेक्षण - (kamalekshan) To the one with lotus-like eyes

यो ब्रह्माणं विदधाति पूर्वं - (yo brahmanam vidadhati purvam) The one who imparts knowledge of the Absolute Truth (Brahman) first
यो वै वेदांश्च प्रहिणोति तस्मै - (yo vai vedamshcha prahinoti tasmai) The one who bestows the Vedas upon beings
तह देवं आत्मबुद्धिप्रकाशं - (taha devam atma-buddhi-prakasham) There, to that God who illuminates the knowledge of the Self
मुमुक्षुर्वै शरणमहं प्रपद्ये - (mumukshur vai sharanam aham prapadye) I, a seeker of liberation, surrender unto Him
