एकांत में बैठ कर - Sitting alone
भगवान का रूपध्यान करते हुए - While meditating on the form of God
उनके नाम - His names
गुण लीलादिकों - His qualities, pastimes, etc.
का गान करना - Singing or chanting

Meaning:
Sitting alone, while meditating on the form of God, one sings or chants His names, qualities, and pastimes.
